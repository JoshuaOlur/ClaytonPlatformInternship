The Advanced Learning Collective

The Advanced Learning Collective serves as a dynamic professional learning and resource network that promotes excellence in gifted education and advanced learning through collaboration, innovation, leadership development, and continuous professional growth.

Established by the Department of Gifted & Advanced Learning, this platform strengthens instructional practice, enhances program implementation, supports leadership capacity, and increases family engagement by providing equitable access to high-quality learning resources and professional development opportunities.

Our Mission

Advancing excellence in gifted and advanced learning through collaboration, innovation, and equitable access.
